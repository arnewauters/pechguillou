about pech guillou

Frequently described as one of the finest houses in the area, Pech Guillou is a medieval hunting lodge. It sits in an elevated position offering breathtaking views which have changed little over the centuries. The traditional stone manoir house offers comfortable accommodation. There are eight bedrooms sleeping 14-17 people, a heated swimming pool, a boules court, a games room and a summer kitchen to make outdoor dining easy.

Of course the house has full central heating, so itâ€™s ideal for use outside the normal holiday season. While winter weather can never be guaranteed, the winters here are usually mild. Longer-term rentals are possible. The layout of the house provides versatile accommodation. Out of season the manoir can be rented for smaller numbers. In addition the house and outbuildings could be used by groups such as artists, photographers, or for activities like yoga, wine courses etc.

Please feel free to contact us to discuss possibilities. We wish you a pleasant stay on our website!
